Olaf, to me; is the most innocent and lovable character in the film Frozen. He has a great comic sense so he is a lot of fun too. And his Greatest Strength is providing "The Comfort Blanket" to his friends; which comes so naturally to him as he is essentially an emotional, sincere, caring and a helpful being. He also has a great temperament. And goes to any lengths for his friends representing his sacrificing nature. If you thought that was all, well..... he is a great Guide too.

All in All, A Happy Soul and I derive such of an inspiration from him.
